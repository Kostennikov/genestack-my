Take the pain out of manual curation

Quickly and easily curate big datasets using scalable GUI tools such as controlled vocabulary powered auto-complete suggestions for invalid entries.

Leverage the potential of both manual and programmatic curation

Manually curate big datasets using scalable GUI tools such as controlled vocabulary powered auto-complete suggestions for invalid entries, or automate curation via scripts to bulk edit multiple studies.

Leverage the power of programmatic curation

Automate curation via scripts and rules files to edit multiple studies in bulk.

Ensure your metadata integrity

Quickly check your metadata to spot inconsistencies using features such as sample table filtering.

A collaborative data ecosystem

We have specific APIs for different user classes, such as data managers, bioinformaticians and discovery scientists, so that everybody can harness the power of multi-omics data in their day to day work.

Amy Tang, PhD, ODM Product Manager

Take control of your metadata

Define your own metadata models using customizable templates to enforce metadata attributes and allowed values, including controlled-vocabulary integration.

Convert legacy data, ensure new data is right from the start

ODM's curation tools mean dealing with legacy data is quick and easy, while new data can be validated on import or created within ODM in compliance with your metadata model.

Curation workflows and publishing to suit your organization

ODM's flexible groups and permissions systems are easy to adapt to your data curation workflow. Set up curator users to work on pre-published datasets before sharing them with the wider organization once they are validated.
